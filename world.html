Heirlings - an Elaboration

Heirlings are beings that are manifested when one experiences an overwhelming emotion. Heirlings were originally objects that held immense emotional value to an individual. the the emotional connection grows to a point, and thus the object gains sentience and becomes an heirling. (meaning, it doesnt necessarily have to be out of love, negative emotions as well.)

They are creatures that have container-like bodies on their horns and torso. These containers would carry a specific filling related to their manifestation and its essence depending on each heirling.

Heirlings are divided by lineages. There is the Core lineage that houses 3 subtypes: Infusion (where fillings are related to 'tea' like substances), Fizzy (where fillings are related to 'soda' like substances) and Culinary (where fillings are related to any food/snack/dessert.)

Each Heirling has their own 'memory box' it is a 'container' of some kind that acts as a sort of portal that allows their heirling to enter their own 'memoryscape'. The memoryscape is basically the time and place of when that heirling is manifested, and is preserved eternally within the memory box. Ex: you get super happy seeing your birthday cake in your bday party, that happiness gets manifested into a heirling, the heirling's memory box could be cake shaped, or look like the box the cake came in, or some other creative interpretation of the memory, since the human got so happy after seeing it. And so the cake would serve as a memory box for the heirling.

Now what is a memory box for? Basically it's like a genie lamp for a heirling, it serves as a portal to the mortal realm and the memoryscape. Each heirlings memory scape is very unique. Going back to the bday cake heir, their memoryscape would be the time and place of when the heir is manifested, so for this heir their memoryscape would pretty much be the birthday party.
